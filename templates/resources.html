Pre-Export Checklist

Before You Submit Your Resume

  • Keep resume focused — make the top third dense with role-relevant content
  • Prefer hard skills, tools, and project outcomes over generic claims
  • Use consistent date formats, job titles, and section names for ATS parsers
  • Save as a text-readable PDF — avoid image-only or scanned resume pages
  • Limit to 1–2 pages; recruiters spend ~7 seconds on the first scan
  • Include quantified achievements in every experience bullet point
Iteration Strategy
